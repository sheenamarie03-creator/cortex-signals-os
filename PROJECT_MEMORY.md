# Cortex Signals / Cortex Insights Project Memory

Founder: Sheena M. Lee, DMSc, PA-C

Purpose:
Cortex Signals is a selective intelligence brief focused on neurotechnology, neurogenetics, neuromodulation, BCI, neurodiagnostics, cognitive and sensory devices, and clinically relevant brain-health tools.

Audience:
Primary: neurotech investors, founders, operators, and commercially minded clinicians.
Secondary: clinicians, educators, analysts, and people trying to understand brain-health innovation.

Voice:
Grounded, clinically informed, discerning, commercially aware, not hype-driven.

Positioning:
Cortex Signals helps smart readers interpret neurotechnology through a clinical and strategic lens.

Current assets:
- Domains: cortexsignals.co, cortexinsights.co, cortexcap.co
- Beehiiv account
- Existing newsletter issues and drafts
- Cortex Signals brand concept
- Clinical background in psychiatry, neurology, IDD, autism, and PA education

Do not build:
- Generic digital health newsletter
- Wellness content
- Consumer mental health content unless it connects to regulated or clinically meaningful brain/behavior technology
- Anything that depends on university-owned course content

Core categories:
- BCI
- neuromodulation
- neurogenetics
- neurodiagnostics
- cognitive assessment
- sensory devices
- digital mental health treatment devices
- neurorehabilitation
- brain-health devices
- neuro-adjacent AI when clinically relevant

Initial product:
Private editorial dashboard + public website.

First build goal:
Create a clean public-facing website and a simple internal source tracker.
